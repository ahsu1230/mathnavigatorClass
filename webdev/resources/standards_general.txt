Standards Guide
General
---------------------

Why is coding standards important?
1. Easier to read for yourself and for others
2. Good organization -> Less complexity -> Reduces errors
https://blog.fossasia.org/why-coding-standards-matter/


////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////
With your current text editor, make sure one tab is 4 spaces.

Not 2 spaces. And not the character \t.


////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////
Comments are good, but too many makes the file look verbose and messy

Avoid writing paragraphs and essays to describe your work.
Usually, clear, concise code won't require comments to be understandable.


////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////
Line Wrapping

Usually, lines over 80 characters are too long to read.
Wrap them by using line breaks.
